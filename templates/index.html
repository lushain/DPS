Dexter's Laboratory

About Us

Sleep tight, America! Your fate lies safely in the hands of Dexter, a child genius who whips up dazzling, world-saving inventions in his secret laboratory. This time his sister Dee Dee wont be around to save us from his hellish new inventions.

His intelligence makes him the brains of the family and he is one of the world's greatest inventors, having created countless weird and amazing machines and experiments. He is always working on new inventions and creations every day, but his sister Dee Dee is always breaking into his lab and destroying his work. The daily events surrounding Dexter's life mainly revolve around his relationship with his sister and everything that happens between the two.

Robo-Dexo 2000


It is a giant robot designed to look like Dexter. It is used on both adventures and in battle, usually to save the world.

Robo-Dexo 3000


Robo-Dexo 3000 is an advanced giant fighting robot Dexter created to replace the older Robo-Dexo 2000. Upon its test on a giant asteroid it was proven to be far superior than its 2000 model

Charlie


Charlie is an experiment of Dexter's. Though he was meant to scare Dee Dee, Charlie seemed to grow an appetite for goats.

Multi-Formic Megabot


Of all the inventions Dexter has created, the Multi-Formic Megabot was one of his greatest - and eventually the last hope in eliminating Badaxtra in Japan in the events of Last But Not Beast.
